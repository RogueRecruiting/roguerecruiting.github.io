About Us


Founded and headquartered in Chandler, AZ, Rogue Recruiting specializes in employment recruiting with an emphasis on the needs of our long standing client relationships. Our passion is fostering relationships with our clients to become an integral part of their success and growth. Rogue recruiters understand the needs for continuity and consistency from your work force and ensure that all of our employees will be as unique as your facilities and work force needs.

Rogue stands by to offer top flight recruiting, retention and staffing solutions that today's high pace working world demands. Your facility will see an improvement in your recruitment process from day one by taking advantage of any of our contract service options; including our one of a kind Weekly Fee Service that ensures top flight candidates as your internal employees and industry leading pricing that mitigates all risk with the candidate you choose.
